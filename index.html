About Delightful Bean

Founded in 2020 by passionate coffee enthusiasts, Delightful Bean started with a simple mission: bringing cafe-quality coffee to outdoor events. What began as a single cart at local festivals has grown into Spring Hill's premier mobile coffee service, powered by our love for exceptional coffee and community gatherings.

Our core values center on quality, sustainability, and community engagement. We source ethically produced beans, use eco-friendly materials, and partner with local events to create memorable experiences. Every cup we serve reflects our commitment to excellence and our passion for bringing people together through outstanding coffee.

Frequently Asked Questions

What areas do you service?

We primarily service the Spring Hill area and surrounding regions within a 50-mile radius. For locations outside this area, additional travel fees may apply. Contact us for specific location availability and pricing.

How many people can you serve?

Our high-capacity setup can serve up to 200 guests per hour. For larger events, we can provide multiple stations or extended service hours to accommodate your needs.

What drinks do you offer?

We offer a full range of espresso-based drinks, cold brew, specialty lattes, and non-coffee alternatives including tea and hot chocolate. Custom menu options are available upon request.

What is your pricing structure?

Our pricing is customized based on event duration, expected attendance, and service requirements. We offer both per-person packages and hourly rates. Contact us for a detailed quote.

How far in advance should we book?

We recommend booking at least 4-6 weeks in advance for large events, though we can sometimes accommodate last-minute requests based on availability.
